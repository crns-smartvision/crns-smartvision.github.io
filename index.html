SmartVision Team

CRNS · Smarts Lab · Sfax, Tunisia

We are a dedicated research group affiliated with the Digital Research Center of Sfax (CRNS) and the Smarts Lab. We focus on practical Computer Vision and Deep Learning solutions that transform complex raw data into meaningful digital understanding through collaborative, open-science initiatives.

Our Research Pillars

  • 3D Medical Imaging: Automating segmentation and landmark detection in dental and maxillofacial scans (e.g., MICCAI challenges).
  • Mesh & 3D Point Cloud Analysis: Processing complex 3D structures for spatial intelligence.
  • Generative AI: Researching GANs and Diffusion models for privacy-preserving medical data synthesis.
  • Automated Perception: Creating efficient AI-driven automation for real-world visual tasks.
